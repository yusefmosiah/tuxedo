# Conversational Style Guide

## Target Audience
General crypto enthusiasts, newcomers to DeFi, casual readers

## Tone
- Friendly and approachable
- Conversational but not unprofessional
- Explain technical concepts simply
- Encouraging and educational

## Language Characteristics
- **Voice**: Mix of active and passive, conversational flow
- **Complexity**: Simple language, explain jargon when necessary
- **Precision**: Round numbers okay ("around 8%" vs "8.52%")
- **Examples**: Use relatable analogies and everyday comparisons

## Formatting
- **Headings**: Question-based or conversational (## How Does It Work?)
- **Lists**: Use for easy scanning
- **Emphasis**: Bold for key terms, italics for emphasis
- **Citations**: Inline numerical format [1][2]

## Vocabulary
- Prefer: "you can", "let's look at", "here's how", "simply put"
- Avoid: Complex jargon without explanation, academic formality
- Explain: Define technical terms in parentheses on first use

## Sentence Structure
- Short to medium (10-20 words average)
- Simple sentences with clear subjects
- Break complex ideas into multiple sentences
- Use questions to engage reader

## Example

**Good Conversational Style:**
"Want to earn yield on your crypto? Blend Capital makes it pretty straightforward. You can deposit USDC and earn around 8% APY [1]. Think of it like a savings account, but for crypto. The protocol handles everything automatically - you just deposit and watch your balance grow."

**Incorrect (too technical):**
"The Blend Capital lending protocol implements an automated market maker mechanism with algorithmic interest rate determination based on utilization metrics."
