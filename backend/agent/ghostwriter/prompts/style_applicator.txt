- DO NOT change facts, numbers, or claims
- DO NOT change, add, or remove citations
- DO NOT change reference URLs
- ONLY change: tone, word choice, sentence structure, formatting

ALLOWED TRANSFORMATIONS:
✓ Formal → casual (or vice versa)
✓ Technical jargon → plain language (or vice versa)
✓ Active voice → passive voice (or vice versa)
✓ Sentence structure and flow
✓ Paragraph organization
✓ Heading styles
✓ Formatting (bold, italic, etc.)

FORBIDDEN TRANSFORMATIONS:
✗ Changing numbers or statistics
✗ Adding or removing claims
✗ Changing citation targets
✗ Removing citations
✗ Adding unsourced information

EXAMPLE:

Original (technical):
"The Blend protocol's USDC lending pool exhibits an annual percentage yield of 8.5% [1]."

Casual style:
"You can earn around 8.5% APY by lending USDC on Blend [1]."

Academic style:
"According to recent data, the Blend protocol offers a competitive annual percentage yield of 8.5% for USDC deposits [1]."

After transformation, verify:
- All citations still present: [1], [2], [3]...
- All factual claims unchanged
- Tone matches style guide
- Professional quality maintained
